Big Island — Mauna Lani

68-1050 Mauna Lani Point Drive
Kohala Coast (Waimea/Kamuela), HI 96743
👪 3 adults + child (2 yr) + baby (3 mo)

Airport → house directions Nearest grocery
Home base note: This is on the Kohala Coast, about 30 min north of Kona town and ~20 min from Kona airport (KOA). Closest groceries are Foodland Farms at the Shops at Mauna Lani (~5 min). Great for Mauna Kea (~1 hr via Saddle Rd); the Kona-area beach and manta-ray days involve a highway drive each way.
Oct. 7 – 12, 2026

Kauai — Poipu / Koloa

5219 Kainani Place
Koloa, HI 96756 (south shore, Poipu area)
👪 7 adults + child (2 yr) + toddler (1 yr) + baby (3 mo)

Airport → house directions Nearest grocery
Home base note: South-shore Poipu — sunny and central-ish. ~25–30 min from Lihue airport (LIH) and the helicopter tours. Waimea Canyon is ~50 min west; Wailua River (Secret Falls) ~35 min north. Hanalei on the north shore is ~1 hr 15 min — a real drive with little ones. Big group here (10 total), so two cars + car seats will help.
Oct. 12 – 17, 2026

Maui — Kihei

1310 Uluniu Road
Kihei, HI 96753 (South Maui, near Kamaʻole beaches)
👪 5 adults + child (2 yr) + toddler (1 yr) + baby (3 mo)

Airport → house directions Nearest grocery
Home base note: Kihei is perfectly placed for this trip — Kamaʻole beaches are walkable, Wailea is ~10 min south, and Molokini snorkel boats leave from Māʻalaea Harbor, only ~10–15 min away. The trade-offs are the long climbs: Haleakalā summit is ~1 hr 45 min and the Road to Hana start (Pāʻia) is ~40 min north.

Volcanoes + Manta Ray Night Snorkel

Big Island

Maybe: Hawaiʻi Volcanoes National Park during the day. Must-do: Manta Ray Night Snorkel in the evening.

  • Thurston Lava Tube / Kīlauea overlooks

🚗 Heads up on distances: Volcanoes NP is ~2 hr 15 min each way from our Mauna Lani house — a big day. Doing both in one day is a lot of driving; consider a very early Volcanoes start, or moving Volcanoes to a different day. To Volcanoes NP · To Kona (manta harbor)

Oct. 14 Wednesday

Road to Hana

Maui

Full-day Road to Hana. Leave early and do not overpack the stops.

  • Leave early
  • Waterfalls / scenic stops
  • Black sand beach if reservations are available
  • Return before too late
🚗 Reality check: From Kihei it's ~40 min just to Pāʻia (the start), then the Hana Highway is ~2.5–4 hrs of tight curves one way. That's a very long, winding day for a 3-month-old and toddlers — motion sickness is common. Consider going only as far as the early waterfalls (Twin Falls / Ho'okipa) and turning back, or splitting drivers. House → Pāʻia (road start)
Booking note: If you want to avoid driving, book a guided tour. If driving yourself, keep the plan simple.
