🌺 Kauai Beach Luau Picks

Near the Poipu stay — book ahead; confirm 2026 show nights.

Auliʻi Lūʻau ★ oceanfront

Kauaʻi's only true oceanfront luau — on the beach lawn at the Sheraton Kauaʻi (Poipu), sunset right behind the dancers. Best match for an "on the beach" night. Usually Mon & Wed (~$170–$220+/adult).

Auliʻi Luau site Map

Havaiki Nui Lūʻau ★ most family-friendly

Grand Hyatt Kauaʻi (Poipu) — pre-show lei-making + hula lessons for the kids, oceanview lawn. Usually Wed & Sat, so Saturday Oct 10 fits perfectly (it's the flexible day). ~$200+/adult.

Map / info

Smith's Family Garden Luau (alt.)

Very relaxed and kid-friendly, but garden/riverside, not beachfront, ~35–40 min north in Wailua. A good rainy-plan backup.

Map

Tip: with two Wednesdays available (Oct 7 arrival night is tight), Saturday Oct 10 is the smoothest luau night — it lines up with the flexible day and lets everyone rest after travel first.
